F.A.Q

  • Can I pay for my exam on crackeracademy.com?

    No. Our web page is affiliated neither with the GMAC nor the LSAC.

  • Does Cracker Academy provide a real-test experience?

    Yes. Regardless of the plan you have picked, you are granted with a number of full practice tests that are functionally similar to real tests. Also, you can build your own practice test, for which you choose the desired number of questions and select those question types that you would like to deal with.

  • How long does it take to get prepared for the GMAT exam using the GMAT Cracker Platform?

    It depends on the experience you have already had before starting your preparation with the GMAT Cracker Platform and on the Prep Plan you have chosen. The duration may vary from 2 months to half a year.

  • How much time per day should I spend on my preparation?

    We recommend all our students to spend no more than 3 hours daily on their preparation.

  • Do the LSAT Cracker Platform and the GMAT Cracker Platform evaluate essay responses?

    Yes. A team of qualified tutors will assess your essays and leave their feedback and recommendations.

  • What is the online tutor support?

    The online tutor support, available in Premium Prep Pack and Supreme Prep Pack, provides you with an opportunity to communicate with tutors through an integrated chat.

  • What is the test generator and how does it work?

    The test generator is an integrated tool that allows you to create your own practice tests. You can select questions to be included in the practice tests in accordance with their type, total number, difficulty level, etc.

  • How do I access the platform after I have paid for it?

    You will receive a notification message with further instructions by email. You will be granted access to the platform on the same day as you pay.

  • Can I distribute the information contained in the platforms?

    No. The information and materials provided by Cracker Academy platforms are copyrighted and will be protected by all legal means

  • Will my progress be deleted immediately after my account has expired?

    No. Your progress will not be deleted within the next 30 days after the expiration date.

  • Do I need to install any extra software to be able to use the platforms?

    No. You only need your Internet connection to use the platforms.

  • What study materials are placed at the disposal of platforms' users?

    Several Cracker textbooks divided into separate PDF-files attached to different topics, video tutorials, and large banks of questions.

  • What is a live 1-to-1 session?

    A live 1-to-1 session is a one-hour conversation with a professional tutor, during which you can discuss entire topics or separate tasks, arrange your study plan, receive a deep insight into GMAT, etc.

  • Can I buy more 1-to-1 sessions?

    Yes. Feel free to contact support to receive more information.

  • Can I use the GMAT Platform to get prepared for the Executive Assessment?

    Yes. The regular GMAT exam and the Executive Assessment test have similar question types and differ only in the number of questions per section and in the number of those sections.
